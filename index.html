Welcome to HapticMind

Experience guided meditation through rhythmic wrist taps, seamlessly integrated with your Apple Watch. Customize your sessions, track your progress, and explore haptic patterns to enhance your mindfulness journey.

Meditation Session

Start a guided meditation with haptic feedback to guide your breathing. Choose your pattern, duration, and follow the rhythmic taps. The interface displays a timer, breathing phase, and Apple Watch connection status, with options to pause or end the session.

Learning Session

Explore and test different haptic patterns to find what suits you best. Each pattern comes with a description and a "Try" button to experience the haptic feedback in real-time, helping you understand how each pattern feels.

[Insert Settings Screenshot]

Settings

Customize your meditation experience by adjusting session duration, haptic strength, and breathing patterns (Box, 4-7-8, or Custom). The settings page also provides information about each pattern to guide your choices.

[Insert Progress Screenshot]

Progress

Track your meditation journey with stats like weekly sessions, total sessions, day streak, average duration, and favorite pattern. A weekly chart visualizes your activity, motivating you to maintain consistency.

Watch App

The Apple Watch app delivers haptic feedback directly to your wrist. View the timer, current breathing phase, and control your session with play/pause and end buttons, all in a compact, user-friendly interface.
